• Service-Link locates, manages, automates, and optimizes mobile field service representative (FSRs) and their tasks. Service-Link specializes optimizing fieldwork activities and processes to improve productivity, effectiveness, and knowledge. Our software provides real-time scheduling, optimization, routing, inventory, dashboarding, AVL, GIS, dispatch and mobile communications.

  • By combining wireless communication with real-time information exchange and global positioning, Service-Link enables utilities to streamline and automate many of the processes associated with field service. The result is improved mobile workforce performance at a lower operating cost. For the typical utility company, two-thirds of employees are field-based. This makes the automation of field personnel and the extension of critical business applications to remote users a key opportunity for productivity improvement and cost reduction. Our primary market is the United States with some presence in Canada and the Caribbean.
